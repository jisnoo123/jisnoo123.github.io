How to Choose Your Font

Serif Fonts (with decorative strokes): Traditional, elegant, and formal. Great for academic or professional websites.

Sans-Serif Fonts (clean and simple): Modern, clean, and easy to read on screens. Perfect for contemporary designs.

Consider:

  • Readability across different devices
  • The personality you want to convey
  • How it pairs with your existing design
  • Professional vs. casual appearance
